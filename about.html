About AgroPredict AI

AgroPredict AI is a climate-smart decision support platform that combines hyper-local weather data, soil science and generative artificial intelligence to deliver actionable guidance to smallholder farmers when they need it most. Our mission is to increase yields, reduce risk and improve food security across Africa.

Founded in 2025 by software engineer Michael Salami and agronomist Hannah Adeyemo, AgroPredict AI brings together deep agronomic expertise and cutting-edge technology to transform agriculture.
